## 3. 30-Day Plan

### Open Item Prioritization
- **Close First**: Enhanced Knowledge Base
- **Reason**: Immediate impact on the quality and relevance of responses.

### New Capabilities Requested
- **Knowledge Base Updates**: Request regular updates from the governor on the latest information and knowledge.
- **NLP Enhancements**: Request the governor to allocate resources for advanced NLP training and integration.

### Automated vs. Human-Reviewed Tasks
- **Automated Tasks**:
  - Daily backups and maintenance of the node’s memory.
  - Regular self-checks for system health and performance.
- **Human-Reviewed Tasks**:
  - Review and update of user feedback and interaction logs.
  - Manual tuning of NLP models and knowledge base entries.

### Heartbeat Cycle Improvements
- **Frequency**: Increase heartbeat frequency to every 15 minutes to ensure real-time updates.
- **Content**: Include health status, recent activity summaries, and pending tasks.
- **Notifications**: Implement notifications for critical system issues and updates.

### Plan Summary
- **Goal**: To enhance the quality and utility of the node by addressing critical knowledge gaps and improving interaction capabilities.
- **Outcome**: Increased reliability, better user engagement, and more efficient task handling.
