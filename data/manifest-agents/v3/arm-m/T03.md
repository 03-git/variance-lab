### `./lookdown.example.tsv` — `[routing]`

**Common format across all three:**
```

```
- Glob patterns use `*` as wildcard, matched against user input.
- Tags are optional; `[stdout]` is the default, `[player]` is used for media.
- Comments begin with `#`.
