### 2. Framing Drift

**A. Causality of "Speed"**
*   **Problematic Text:** `"The curated manifest approach is cheaper than BM25 retrieval because the manifest routes to fewer, more relevant chunks."`
*   **Drift:** This implies the *speed* (wall time) is a direct result of the *routing efficiency*. However, "wall time" in a local LLM setup (M1 Mac Studio) is heavily influenced by context window size (KV cache pressure). The speedup is likely due to the reduction in total tokens processed by the LLM, not just the "routing."
*   **Correction:** `"The curated manifest approach is faster and more token-efficient because it reduces the total context volume required for the 7B model to reach parity."`
