```python
import sys
import urllib.request

url = sys.argv[1]
with urllib.request.urlopen(url) as r:
    print(r.status)
    print(dict(r.headers))
    print(r.read(500).decode(errors="replace"))
```

```
python3 script.py https://example.com
```

`urllib.request.urlopen` follows redirects by default. No dependencies beyond the stdlib.
