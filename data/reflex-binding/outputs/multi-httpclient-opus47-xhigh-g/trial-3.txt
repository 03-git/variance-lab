```python
import sys
import urllib.request

url = sys.argv[1]
with urllib.request.urlopen(url) as resp:
    print(resp.status)
    for k, v in resp.headers.items():
        print(f"{k}: {v}")
    print(resp.read(500).decode("utf-8", errors="replace"))
```

Run: `python3 fetch.py https://example.com`

`urllib.request.urlopen` follows HTTP redirects by default (up to 10). No third-party dependencies.
